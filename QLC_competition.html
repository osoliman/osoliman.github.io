Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I participate virtually in the preliminary exams?
Yes! The preliminary exams can be taken either in-person or virtually via Zoom. This option is available to make it easier for participants coming from out of state. The Zoom link will be shared with all registered participants.
Q: Do I need to attend the finals in person?
Yes, the finals will be held in-person at the Islamic Center of New England in Sharon, MA. Only the preliminary exams offer a virtual option.
Q: How will I receive updates about the competition?
All registered participants will receive updates via email, including exam times, Zoom links for virtual participants, and other important information.
